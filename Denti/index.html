About us

Welcome to Denti

Remember 80´s, when pain on the dentist´s chair was the stuff of legends, jokes and nightmares? It was then that Dr. Karve launched Dentech with a vision- Dental Care, the gentle way. Dentech went on to change the way Thanekars looked at dental treatments. Dentech made those smiles perfect, took teeth back to their original, strong selves, gave teeth the care they deserve. And it took care to reduce the pain to minimum and gave high priority to the patient´s comfort.

These values were written in Dentech´s DNA and has held us in good stead over past 28 years. Today, we are Thane and Mumbai´s leading chain of Super Speciality Dental clinics with a team of more than 40 highly qualified and trained dentists spread over five flourishing clinics across Mumbai and Thane. Every branch is equipped with cutting edge technology, at par with the global standards. Our treatments are digitalised with Oral Scanners and dental crowns are designed using CAD (computer assisted design) and CAM (computer assisted manufacturing) technology. We are also equipped with RVG and OPG.

Patient´s Personal details and medical history along with their pictures are digitally stored in our database. So that everything is on our fingertips whenever you come back for future dental treatments and routine follow-ups.

Dr. Vivek Karve

A visionary who created a trailblazing chain of clinics that care. A technocrat who has travelled the world in his pursuit of latest technology that will bring comfort and cheer to his patients´ lives. A leader who has led his team by example. Dr. Karve has spread smiles across Thane with his column ´Smiles of Thane´ in Times of India. His column ran for three years, bringing hope and advancing the cause of oral health and hygiene. His vision of health is not limited to Dental health. He is an internationally certified Nautilus trainer (Fitness Consultant) who takes personal fitness workshops and is currently writing a much-anticipated book on fitness. Dr. Karve is also a marathon runner.

Dental Implant

Dental implants are artificial tooth roots that are surgically placed into the jawbone to support a replacement tooth or bridge. They are made of titanium or a titanium alloy, which is biocompatible and can fuse with the surrounding bone tissue, creating a strong and durable foundation for the replacement teeth.
